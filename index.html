FAQ

Good questions

What exactly is a "Draft"?
A Draft is how you show real interest — an athlete Drafts a program, a coach Drafts a prospect. When both sides Draft each other, that's the handshake: chat unlocks and the recruiting conversation starts.
How much does it cost?
Free to start, forever. Every account gets 20 Drafts a month with unlimited browsing and passing. Starter ($7/mo) and Pro ($15/mo) unlock unlimited Drafts and extra visibility.
My kid plays little league. Can they join?
Yes — athletes from age 6 and up are welcome. Every athlete under 18 needs a parent or guardian to link to their account and approve it before it goes live, and guardians keep oversight of who reaches out.
How do I know a recruiter is real?
Every adult on GetDraft passes government-ID verification before they can connect with anyone. Verified profiles carry a checkmark — and unverified accounts simply can't start conversations.
Which sports are supported?
We're launching football-first in the U.S., with basketball, soccer, hockey, baseball, volleyball and track already in the app. Your sport picks your card style — your grind does the rest.
When can I get the app?
We're in final testing on Google Play, with iOS right behind. Join the waitlist below and you'll get the download link the day we go live.
